On the Subject of The Botany Book

Plant nerd? Really? Wh- Ohhhhh... alchemy.

Henbane

The Henbane is a plant with bell-shaped pale yellow flowers, with purple veins. They grow up to 36 inches in height. The leaves are hairy and sticky to the touch.
It's alchemical effect is that it causes hallucinations, due to its poisonous nature.

Belladonna

The Belladonna is a plant with bell-shaped flowers, brown or brown-purple in hue. It's berries are around the size of a cherry, changing from green to black. The berries have a green star-shaped 'foot'.
It's alchemical effect is an increase in vision ability, despite it's poisonous nature. It's leaves can also be used to enhance a potion's effect.

Mandrake

The Mandrake is a stemless herb with large roots that can grow up to 2 feet long. Flowers emerge from a central cluster, and range in color. It's sweet-smelling fruits resemble a smaller yellow apple.
It's alchemical effect is causing affection.
